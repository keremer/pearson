\# Professional Development

\*\*Course Code\*\*: PD-01

\*\*Level\*\*: HND Art \& Design Level 4

\*\*Instructor\*\*: Dr. Kerem ERCOSKUN

\*\*Language\*\*: English / Turkish

\*\*Delivery\*\*: Weekly seminars, workshops, portfolio sessions

\## Course Aim

The aim of this unit is to provide students with the opportunity to define areas for personal professional development within the context of the broad scope of the creative industries. The course emphasizes self-development, building an intellectual background, and attending social and professional activities to enhance students' professional identity, networks, and employability.

\## Learning Outcomes

\- \*\*LO1\*\*: Explore the creative industries professions, through research into historic and contemporary precedent

\- \*\*LO2\*\*: Discuss personal career goals in relation to the range of roles and subjects in the creative industries

\- \*\*LO3\*\*: Define personal development plans, highlighting areas to support specific career goals and general skills

\- \*\*LO4\*\*: Critically reflect on the achievement of personal development goals and plan for the future

\## Weekly Structure

| Week | Topic | Activity | Assignment |

|------|-------|----------|------------|

| 1 | Introduction to Professional Identity | Group discussion | Define your career goals and create a brief personal development plan |

| 2 | Sector Mapping | Research \& presentation | SWOT analysis of chosen sector |

| 3 | Communication and Networking Skills | Role play | Analyze your communication style and report on a professional networking event |

| 4 | Time Management and Productivity | Workshop | Track daily activities and create a prioritized to-do list |

| 5 | Studio Setup \& Ergonomics | Sketching \& layout | Visual studio map |

| 6 | Stress Management and Work-Life Balance | Seminar | Keep a stress diary and develop a personal plan to improve work-life balance |

| 7 | Leadership and Teamwork Skills | Group activity | Reflect on a past team experience and analyze team dynamics |

| 8 | Problem Solving and Decision Making | Case study analysis | Apply a decision-making model to a hypothetical professional scenario |

| 9 | Professional Ethics and Responsibility | Seminar | Identify an ethical dilemma in your profession and propose a solution |

| 10 | Personal SWOT, Career Planning and Goal Setting | Individual reflection | Personal SWOT document and a 1, 3, and 5-year personal career plan |

| 11 | CV \& Portfolio | Workshop | Draft CV and portfolio cover |

| 12 | Digital Setup, AI Tools \& Continuous Learning | Demo \& checklist | AI-generated visual, reflection, and personal learning plan |

| 13 | Interview Simulation \& Freelance vs Corporate | Mock interview \& comparative analysis | Write an interview scenario and a career path comparison |

| 14 | Final Project | Presentation \& reflection | Career strategy presentation |

\## Assessment Formats

\- Written Assignment: Personal Development Plan outlining short-term and long-term career goals based on industry analysis

\- Portfolio: Curated collection of professional tools including a CV, portfolio cover, and visual studio map

\- Presentation: 15-minute pitch of the final career strategy evaluating personal SWOT and sector mapping
