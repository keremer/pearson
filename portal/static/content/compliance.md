# Digital Data Integrity & Regulatory Compliance Statement (2026)

This portal serves as the authoritative **Master Material List (MML)** and **Technical Specification Registry** for projects managed under the **CRM in AEC** framework. To ensure seamless interoperability and legal validity in the digital construction ecosystem, all data hosted here adheres to the following standards:

### 1. Turkish National Digital Regulation (Ek-5 & Ek-6)
In strict accordance with the **"Mimarlık ve Mühendislik Projelerinin Dijital Olarak Hazırlanması Hakkında Yönetmelik"**:
* **Object Classification (Ek-5):** Every work item is assigned a mandatory 3-character **Kategori Kodu** (e.g., KOL, PER, DKV) to ensure correct identification within the national digital proejct infrastructure.
* **BIM Interoperability (Ek-6):** All entries are mapped to their legally required **IFC Class** (Industry Foundation Classes), facilitating automated quality control and model validation.
* **Naming Conventions:** Material identifiers are optimized for the 15-character constraint mandated for digital project submissions.

### 2. International Standardization
To support global tendering and international partnerships:
* **UniClass 2015:** Our registry is synchronized with the **January 2026 UniClass update**, providing a globally recognized classification for work results and products.
* **CSI MasterFormat 2020:** The technical hierarchy maintains alignment with North American specification standards for broad industry compatibility.

### 3. Bilingual Technical Clarity
Recognizing the international nature of modern AEC projects, every specification is provided in a dual-language format (**Turkish / English**). This ensures that technical requirements are interpreted accurately by all stakeholders, from local subcontractors to global investors, reducing risk and increasing project transparency.
